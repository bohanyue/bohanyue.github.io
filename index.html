Hey 👋 I’m Bohan Yue.

I am currently a 3rd year PhD student at the Institute for Astronomy, University of Edinburgh. My research interest is understanding the cosmic evolution of radio quasars, in particular through statistical tools that separate AGNs and their host galaxies from quasar radio emission. I work with multi-wavelength data including LOFAR and WEAVE. My supervisors are Prof. Philip Best, Dr. Ken Duncan and Prof. Huub Röttgering.
